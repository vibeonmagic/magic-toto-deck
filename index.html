Partnership
What we sign

A simple agreement, designed to make the partnership effortless on your side. Magic operates the Fanverse; you keep your fans, your data, and your brand.

Name & image licence StandardRight to display ElGrandeToto's brand, visuals and name in building and operating the Fanverse.
Fanverse link in bioArtist adds the Magic Fanverse link in his Instagram / TikTok / YouTube bio for the duration of the contract.
Social push commitmentMinimum 2 posts/month on social networks pushing Fanverse activations to the artist's audience.
Revenue split Key termsIn-app purchases & premium subs: 50% Magic / 50% artist. Merch & ticketing: Magic takes 10% commission on volumes driven through the Fanverse. Streaming: 100% artist.
Fan data ownership YoursFan data generated in the Fanverse is ElGrandeToto's first-party data. Magic is processor, not controller.
Concert anchor clauseAll milestones indexed to the confirmed March 27 concert date. If it shifts, the agreement shifts accordingly.
Term & exit12-month initial term. On exit, fan data is fully transferred to the artist. No lock-in.
